100 Men on a Mission:

Golf Outing

Our 2nd annual outing has been booked so mark those calendars for Thursday, September 29th, 12:30 p.m. shotgun start, 11:30 a.m. registration opens.

We'll be back at Beaver Creek Golf Club and we have once again selected Single Parent Provision to be the recipient of our fundraising.

Sponsor

We are actively seeking sponsors and if that's you, look no further!

View the Sponsor Flyer to check out how you can help.

Sign Up

Have you secured yourself a foursome? Registration is open!

Click the Sign Up button and with a few keystrokes you will be set.

Prizes galore and Chick-Fil-A will once again be providing a fantastic lunch so come hungry.

If you need any final motivation, read what Tim Kramer, team captain of the defending champs has to say: "It was such an honor to compete with not only additional great golfers, but great men! The turnout for the first year was electrifying which made the tournament a blast to play in. We can't wait to have an opportunity to golf with the men from last year as well as new individuals so we can reach even more charities and continue to be a local force in philanthropy in central Iowa."

There you have it men, game on!
