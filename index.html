100+ Men on a Mission

We are a group of local men who are committed to supporting our local community in Des Moines, Iowa.

We want to reach out and help our community by finding 100 or more men to each contribute four times a year to give to local Des Moines Area charities.

Whether it being a financial donation or a donation of time and energy, 100+ Men on a Mission wants to help build up the communities of the Des Moines area.

Local charities and projects help build a stronger community.
